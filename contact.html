Sophie offers a free and relaxed wedding flower consultation, to discuss ideas and show you examples of her work. She is based on the South coast and covers Bournemouth, Poole, the New Forest and surrounding areas. For your consultation, it would help if you have details of your dresses, colour scheme and any ideas or photos of flowers that may have caught your eye. Sophie will work closely with you to achieve the perfect flowers within your budget for your special day.
